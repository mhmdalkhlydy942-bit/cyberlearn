What is Phishing?

Phishing is the practice of sending fraudulent communications that appear to come from a legitimate source, usually to steal money or sensitive information or to deliver malware.

How Phishing Works

Attackers craft convincing emails or messages that lure victims into clicking links, opening attachments, or entering credentials on fake websites.

Prevention Tips

  1. Don't click unknown links
  2. Verify sender addresses
  3. Use MFA and strong passwords
